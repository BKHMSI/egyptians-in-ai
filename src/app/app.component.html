Egyptians in AI

Welcome to Egyptian in AI, a website dedicated to showcasing the profiles of prominent Egyptian researchers in the field of artificial intelligence. If you think that someone is missing from our list of featured researchers, we welcome contributions from the community. To be eligible for consideration to be added to our list, the only requirement is that the individual has an h-index of 5 or higher as displayed on their Google Scholar profile. You can fill this form to suggest the addition of a new researcher or to update the information for an existing one. We hope that you find our website informative and inspiring, and we invite you to explore the profiles of our featured researchers to learn more about their work. The list is sorted by h-index.
